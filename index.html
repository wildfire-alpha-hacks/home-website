We’re a Worldwide Organization.

Pledge Hope is a global 501(C)3 organization that contains volunteers who share one common goal: to pledge hope to individuals around the world. Pledge Hope aims to give back to the community through its four pillars, each of which target a different issue that is currently facing the world: health, occupation, planet, and education.
