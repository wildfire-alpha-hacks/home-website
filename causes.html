Health Pillar

The health pillar is where we raise the awareness of different disorders and plan fundraisings and donate to different organizations for certain disorders. Join us and you will get the opportunities to work with doctors around the world and use your knowledge to give a hand in defeating these disorders. The health team has weekly meetings that are mandatory, to discuss the progress of different projects. Make sure to check it out if interested!

Occupation Pillar

This pillar helps undergraduates in the society. We work with partnering companies to help them to find the jobs they deserve, the best match for our candidates. Learning how to browse different websites and building knowledge in the business field are also part of our pillar. It is in your hand to help them! Apply for this pillar if you feel like you have knowledge about the occupation industry and want to help underprivileged college grads!

Plant Pillar

The idea started with the avocado core, which people usually throw away in the end. What we do is grow them instead, or any other seeds of any other fruits. We grow them and when they reach a certain size, we will take them to our local store and sell them. The money that returns to us will be further donated to help with current societal issues. You don’t need to have gardening experiences or knowledge, the plants we grow are all very easy!

Education Pillar (International)

For this pillar, you volunteer as tutors to help any non-US high schoolers that want to pursue their college education in the United States with their TOEFL (test of English as a foreign language) and SAT or ACT tests. But you will have to be trained and passed the test before you start as a tutor, but don’t worry! We will provide you the materials! There is also a cultural exchange program in the international aspect if you want to share your culture!

Education Pillar (Domestic)

For the domestic pillar, we help the US immigrants to learn the casual ways of speech, something the textbook won’t teach them. All you have to do is have a casual conversation so they can learn the small details from you. These are great ways to make new friendships, whether outside or across the country. Mentoring elementary and middle schoolers are also part of this pillar! If you are interested in mentoring and tutoring, please join!

Teams

We also have multiple teams that you can be be a part of through Pledge Hope. From marketing to social media to the technology team, there are a wide variety of options to choose from! If you're interested in looking specifically at more teams, be sure to check out the teams page on the website by clicking the button below!
