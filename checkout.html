College Preparation

Learn the strategies of a stellar college application and develop a plan of conduct for your high school years. You’ll learn how to find your niche, stand out to college admission officers, and how to get recognized and earn research positions in your area. We will even assist you individually in finding programs, building resumes, and how to make an impact in your community! We will even offer study plans for standardized tests, how to best utilize resources available already, and the best financial aid and scholarship programs.

Exploring Business

Explore the advantages and challenges of creating and scaling your own enterprise in the Exploring Business Course. Our two-week virtual program takes an in-depth look at choosing a start-up, prototype testing, market research, social innovation, global business, and the operations of running your own business. You will have opportunities to virtually hear from and interact with experienced entrepreneurs across different industries to learn about what it takes to become successful in any career path.

Software Engineering Fundamentals

Gain a deeper understanding of the underlying context and theory of software development practices. This course is designed for people who are new to software engineering. During our two-week long program, students will be able to:
1.) Build high-quality and secure software using SDLC methodologies such as agile, lean, and traditional/waterfall.
2.) Analyze a software development team's SDLC methodology and make recommendations for improvements.
3.) Compare and contrast software development methodologies with respect to environmental, organizational, and product constraints.

Health Enrichment

Explore the details of the vast field of health, including the complex anatomy of the human body and the work of a wide variety of health professionals. In this two week course, you’ll learn about human anatomy by competing in a model building competition against other students (The best models will win prizes!). Learn about the work of different medical and health professionals, including physicians, doctors, and nurses through their presentations or by speaking with them.

Working in small groups, you will build your teamwork and leadership skills while also having fun competing against other teams in trivia games like jeopardy. Test your own knowledge by examining and trying to solve interesting case studies. Focused on giving you a comprehensive experience of the field of health, our program will provide you with hands-on activities, like learning how to suture, to simulate the feeling of being a healthcare professional.
